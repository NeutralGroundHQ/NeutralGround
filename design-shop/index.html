The Design Shop

Where strategy becomes something you can hold.

The Design Shop isn't a meeting. It's a designed experience — a structured environment where your team's best thinking gets captured, organized, and built into something real.

The Collaboration Code

The methodology behind Neutral Ground's work is grounded in the principles of The Collaboration Code — co-authored by Jonah Evans and his father, Rob Evans of Imaginal Labs. The book articulates the theory and practice of designing collaborative environments where hard challenges get solved.
