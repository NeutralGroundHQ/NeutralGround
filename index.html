What We Do

We make complexity navigable.

Most alignment efforts begin in the room. By then, it is already late. Perspectives are filtered, time is limited, and conversation is shaped as much by group dynamics as by the ideas themselves.


Neutral Ground works differently. We start by listening — deeply, privately, and at scale — before anyone enters the room. Then we design the conversations that actually move groups forward.


The result is not just better conversations. It's faster alignment, stronger decisions, and momentum that lasts beyond the room.

01

Deep Listening at Scale

We hear from your people — one voice at a time, privately — before any group conversation. What they won't say in a meeting, they'll say to us.

02

AI-Powered Synthesis

mosAIc synthesizes what your organization actually thinks, knows, and needs into a clear, honest picture. No black boxes. No replacement of human judgment.

03

Expert Facilitation

We design and facilitate conversations that turn insight into strategy, alignment into decisions, and decisions into action — compressed from months into days.

04

Strategic Design

Through The Design Shop, we help organizations build the structures, stories, and systems to move from where they are to where they want to go.

Our Approach

Why our facilitation doesn't suck

— 01

We start before the room

Most facilitators show up on the day. We start weeks earlier — listening, mapping the terrain, designing a process that reflects what's actually true. By the time the group gathers, we're not starting from scratch.

— 02

We create genuine candor

People say things in a private conversation that they will never say in a meeting or to their boss. We create that space deliberately. The result isn't polite agreement — it's the real picture.

— 03

We design for decisions

Every session is designed around clear outcomes: a decision made, a direction chosen, a commitment owned. We stay until the work is done — not until the time runs out.

— 04

AI accelerates, humans lead

mosAIc lets us listen to dozens of people and synthesize at scale. But every insight is read, considered, and carried forward by experienced human practitioners. No automation of authority.

— 05

We work with you, not at you

We don't arrive with answers. We design processes with your team, train your language into our tools, and adapt in real time. The insights belong to your organization.

— 06

People support what they create

The most durable alignment comes from a process where real perspectives shaped the outcome. That's the difference between a strategy that gets implemented and one that sits in a binder.

"The best facilitation is invisible. The group leaves feeling like they did it themselves — because they did."

— Jonah Evans, Founder & CEO, Neutral Ground

Our Values

What we believe about how this work should go.

01

People support what they help create

The most durable alignment comes from a process that reflects real perspectives. We build that in from the start — not as a nice idea, but as the architecture of everything we do.

02

Hard truth, held well

The most useful thing we can offer a leadership team is an honest picture of where they actually are. We create the conditions for candor — and we don't flinch from what we find.

03

Human judgment, always

AI helps us move fast and synthesize at scale. But every insight is read, considered, and carried forward by experienced human practitioners. We don't automate authority.

04

Design over default

Good outcomes don't happen by accident. They're designed — with clear objectives, thoughtful structure, and a process that respects both the people and the complexity of the challenge.

05

Outcomes, not optics

We measure our work by what changes after we leave — decisions made, strategies implemented, organizations that move. A beautiful process that produces no forward motion is not a success.

06

Neutral ground is a practice

We don't take sides. We create the conditions for groups to find their own way forward — with discipline, trust, and a genuine belief that the people in the room have what they need.

Jonah Evans

About Jonah

Jonah Evans is the Founder and CEO of Neutral Ground, a New Orleans–based firm focused on helping organizations align, decide, and move forward on their most complex challenges.

With more than two decades of international experience, Jonah has worked with 27 of the Fortune 100 companies, federal agencies, global foundations, and nonprofit coalitions to align stakeholders, surface hard truths, and move groups toward meaningful outcomes.

He is the creator of mosAIc — an AI-enabled listening and synthesis approach that combines human facilitation with advanced technology to capture perspectives across systems, revealing patterns, tensions, and opportunities that would otherwise remain hidden.

He holds a master's degree from the Harvard Kennedy School, where he was awarded the Ash Center Fellowship for Democratic Governance and Innovation. He lives in New Orleans with his wife and three children.
